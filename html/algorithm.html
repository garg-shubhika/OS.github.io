GENERAL ALGORITHM

AS AN ATTACKER:

  1. The robot goes to a searching position
  2. Then it starts looking for a ball
    1. If it finds the ball, the robot moves towards it and picks it.
    2. Then it goes to the throwing position.
    3. And the robot throws the ball into the basket.
  3. If the robot does not find a ball from its position, it moves to another position.
  4. Repeat the above steps to score points.


GENERAL FLOWCHART:

To ensure that the robot functions correctly, we made several tests and tried various values of the speed, time, angle and positions.
